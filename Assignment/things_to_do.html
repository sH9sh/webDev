Things To Do


  • Croagh Patrick. Climb the famous reek and take in views of Clew bay.

  • Clew Bay Bike Trail

  • Westport House and Pirate Adventure Park.

  • Live traditional music from Matt Molloy's.

  • Experience A Road to Remember Tour, a multi-stop tour unlocking the Famine of West Mayo; exploring the history, folklore, tradition, songs, and stories of the Great Famine against the backdrop of the stunning scenery of Clew Bay south.
